FOSSEE Optimization Toolbox

  • fminbnd — Solves a multivariable optimization problem on a bounded interval
  • fmincon — Solves a multivariable constrained optimization problem.
  • fminunc — Solves a multivariable unconstrained optimization problem
  • fortran_sum — sum from fortran
  • intlinprog — Solves a mixed integer linear programming constrained optimization problem in intlinprog format.
  • intquadprog — Solves a linear quadratic problem.
  • linprog — Solves a linear programming problem.
  • quadprog — Solves a linear quadratic problem.
  • quadprogmat — Solves a linear quadratic problem.
